Dutch Card Game

Our flagship online card game.

Play online

Play the Dutch Card Game in your browser at dutch.reignofplay.com.

  • Play at dutch.reignofplay.com

How it works

Draw, play, or match the last card's rank (even out of turn). Queens peek at cards; Jacks swap two cards. In Clear and collect, collect all four of your rank from the discard to win. Otherwise, empty your hand or have the fewest points when someone calls final round.
