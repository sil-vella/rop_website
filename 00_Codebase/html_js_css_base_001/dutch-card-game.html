Dutch Card Game

Our flagship card game. Traditional rules, modern play—perfect for family and friends. Play to empty your hand or have the fewest points when someone calls Dutch.

How it works

Players are dealt cards, draw and discard, and can play matching ranks out of turn. Special cards like Queens and Jacks have powers. Call Dutch to start the final round—lowest points (and fewer cards if tied) wins.

Highlights

  • Classic Dutch card game rules
  • 2–6 players, great for groups
  • Queen and Jack special powers
  • Jokers and Red King score zero
